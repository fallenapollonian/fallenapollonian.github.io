## Innovation

Innovation primarily comes from universities and federal laboratories, and is primarily collaborative [(Block & Keller, 2008;](https://itif.org/files/Where_do_innovations_come_from.pdf)  [Stevens et al., 2011)](https://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/nejmsa1008268).

Patent rights have a positive effect on innovation [(Panda & Sharma, 2019)](https://0x0.la/u/K9fgvb6.pdf).
